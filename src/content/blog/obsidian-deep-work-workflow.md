After trying every productivity system out there — GTD, Zettelkasten, bullet journaling — I landed on something that actually sticks.

## The Core Loop

Every morning starts with a daily note. Three things I want to accomplish. Any meetings. A link to yesterday's note for context.

## Obsidian as a Second Brain

The graph view is a gimmick. What matters is frictionless capture and fast retrieval. I keep every note in one flat vault, rely on search, and use backlinks sparingly.

## Weekly Review

Sunday evenings, 30 minutes. I process every inbox item, close open loops, and plan the week ahead. This single habit has done more for my output than any app or framework.
